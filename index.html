An enthusiastic and curious survey research professional with more than 16 years of experience providing insights and a well-balanced split among Consumer research, Development research, and Agro research experience.

My strength includes Team Playing, a strong sense of Planning, Business Development, and Public Relationships.

I started my professional career in 2006 as a Research Executive and was responsible for Market and Development Research at Org-Quest Research Limited. I was leading the Market Research and Banking Research wing at the organization.

In January 2011, I started working in different roles for The Nielsen Company (Bangladesh) Limited. During my tenure at Nielsen, I led diversified business units, including Telecom, Opinion Poll, and FMCG.

In 2015 I was given the responsibility to develop the Agro Research wing at Nielsen.

Later in 2016, I joined a new research agency in Bangladesh named Parlance Consulting Services Limited and led the quantitative research team.

In March 2020, I returned to Org-Quest Research Limited and joined as a Research Director.

Finally, in December 2022, I joined Hansa Marketing Services Limited under Hansa Research Group. In the last 16 years, I designed, implemented, and managed research projects in different market and development research areas. My specialty includes quantitative research, and I have worked in FMCG, Tobacco, Automotive, Telecom, Construction Materials, Opinion Poll, and many more industries.
